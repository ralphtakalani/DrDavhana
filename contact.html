FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I book an appointment?

You can book an appointment by calling our office, using our online booking system, or sending us a WhatsApp message. We recommend booking at least 2-3 days in advance.

Do you accept walk-ins?

We accept walk-ins, but priority is given to patients with appointments. We recommend booking in advance to avoid wait times.

What is your cancellation policy?

Please notify us at least 24 hours in advance if you need to cancel or reschedule. Late cancellations may incur a fee.

Do you offer telemedicine consultations?

Yes, we offer virtual consultations via video call for follow-up visits and certain conditions. Please specify when booking.
