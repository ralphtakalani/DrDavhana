Common Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our services

Do I need a referral to book an appointment?

No, you do not need a referral to see Dr. Davhana. We welcome all patients directly. Referrals are only needed if your medical aid requires one for specialist visits.

How long does a typical consultation take?

Standard consultations are scheduled for 15-20 minutes. For more complex issues or annual check-ups, we allocate 30-45 minutes to ensure thorough care.

Do you offer telemedicine consultations?

Yes, we offer virtual consultations for follow-up visits, medication reviews, and certain conditions where physical examination isn't necessary.

What should I bring to my first appointment?

Please bring your ID, medical aid card, list of current medications, and any relevant medical records or test results.
