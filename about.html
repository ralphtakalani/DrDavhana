Meet Dr. Davhana MG

Dr. Davhana MG is a highly respected General Practitioner with over 12 years of clinical experience in family medicine. She is known for her compassionate approach, attention to detail, and commitment to staying at the forefront of medical advancements.

After completing her medical degree at the University of Pretoria, Dr. Davhana pursued specialized training in Family Medicine, earning her Fellowship from the College of Family Physicians. She has since dedicated her career to providing comprehensive primary care to individuals and families across Gauteng.
